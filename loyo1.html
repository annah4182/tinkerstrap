Tteokbokki
AKA Spicy Rice Cake

Tteokbokki (also spelled ddukbokki, ddeokbokki, dukbokki or topokki) is a beloved Korean rice cake dish with many variations and a rich history. Literally translated as “stir-fried rice cake”, tteokbokki is made with garaetteok, a cylinder-shaped white rice cake. This spicy rice cake dish is enormously popular as a street food and also often enjoyed at home. The spicy, slightly sweet, and chewy rice cakes are simply addictive!
